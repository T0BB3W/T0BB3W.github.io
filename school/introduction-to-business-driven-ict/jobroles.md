# Database Administrator

Principal database administrator at Intelex, I found this job on indeed.com, described as follows: “As a Principal Database Administrator at Intelex, you will lead the scalability, performance, and security of our mission-critical database infrastructure. You will be responsible for designing, optimizing, and automating database solutions that power our multi-tenant SaaS platform, ensuring high availability, zero-downtime migrations, and enterprise-grade reliability.”

This position seems to be for more senior database administrators, requiring a master’s degree, and preferably 10+ years of experience in the field, with an expertise in MS SQL Servers. Experience in Oracle and PostgreSQL is also beneficial, but not mandatory.

Key responsibilities:

- Database Architecture & Scalability

- Performance Optimization & Monitoring

- Security & Compliance

- Automation & CI/CD for Databases

- Cross-Platform Expertise & Cloud Strategy

- Collaboration & Leadership
