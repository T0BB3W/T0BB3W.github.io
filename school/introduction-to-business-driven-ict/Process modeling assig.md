# From Lead to Won Opportunity
# Process Description

Roles in sales:

- Salesperson, performs activities like creating leads, logging calls, scheduling meetings and creating tasks.

- Sales manager, oversees the sales process, monitors lead conversion rates, opportunity progress, and ensures teams meet their targets.

- Marketing team, generates leads and works with sales to qualify leads before they are worked by salespeople.

Roles in configuring the system:

- Salesforce admin, configures the salesforce platform, setting up lead and opportunity fields, managing user roles and permissions.

- Business analyst, works with stakeholders to define requirements for the sales process, such as what fields are required for leads or how opportunities should track products and activities.

Decisions made during the sales process:

- Lead qualification

- Activity planning

- Lead conversion

- Opportunity management

- Closing

Decisions in system config:

- Field customization

- Sales process definition

- User access

- Product configuration

# Order to Cash Process
# Process Description

Roles in the Order to Cash Process:

- Salesperson, initates the process by creating or managing the sales orders based on customers requirements, and displays customer master data as well as monitors the document flow to track order status.

- Order management specialist, displays and manages customer orders to ensure accurate details eg. Products, quantities and delivery dates. Also coordinates with customers and internally to resolve order issues.

- Warehouse, handles outbound delivery documents to prepare and ship goods to customers. Ensures the deliveries match the sales order and keeps delivery status up to date.

- Billing clerk, displays billing documents to verify invoices for the customers, ensuring billing accuracy.

- Accounts receivable specialist, monitors payment receipt from customers based on billing documents as well as analysis of document flow to ensure the process is complete.

- SAP Admin, configures and maintains the SAP system for order to cash processes, including customer master data, sales order types, delivery and billing settings.

- SAP Dev, Customizes SAP functionality, for example creating custom reports for document flow analysis or automation of billing processes.

- Business Analyst, works with admins to align SAP configurations with business needs, such as defining order to cash requirements.

Decision making in the Order to Cash process:

- Customer verification

- Order creation and validation

- Delivery scheduling and execution

- Billing accuracy

- Document flow analysis

Decision making in system configuration:

- Data field selection

- Process automation

- Access control

- Document flow customization
