# What did you perform and learn during the introductory excercises 0a and 0b?

I read the documentation, turned on the azure labs machine, remotely accessed it from my laptop using the host information through the “Windows app” which can be downloaded from the app store on macOS. The connection opened in a whole new desktop, which is very handy for me since the gestures to get out of the desktop that’s open is something I use often and doesn’t interfere with the vm’s functionality. Having the desktop launch on an external docked screen also makes the experience much smoother, did not have to toy around with any of the scaling or font sizes since the vm fills the whole screen.
