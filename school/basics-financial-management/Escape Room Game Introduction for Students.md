# Escape Room Game Introduction for Students


## Slide 1: Financial Management Escape Room Game



## Slide 2: What is it about?

A group of players solves various tasks using hints, clues, and their reasoning skills.

The players are locked in a room, and by solving the constructed tasks, they find a key or a door code, allowing them to progress from one task to another and eventually get out of the room.

The game has a time limit. (45 min) Not everyone will manage to get out of the room, and the time used can also be measured, so solving the tasks brings fame and glory.

The groups will report their results on the escape room's Instagram.

Escape room partners: K-Group, Krea, InnoLab



## Slide 3: Escape Room Game and Financial Management?

Both in financial management and in the escape room game, numbers are involved.

Solving the codes for number locks by calculating financial management tasks is a great way to get motivated to solve calculations correctly.

Skills needed in working life include, among others, problem-solving ability, creativity, and teamwork skills.

-> In the escape room game, students can practice precisely these skills together as a team.



## Slide 4: The Story of the Game in Brief:

The events of the game take place in the office of a hardware store.
Jussi Juntunen is a high-level finance professional and a skeptical, old-school man who has yet to transition into the digital age.

Jussi fears corporate Spies like the plague. He has hidden passwords, price lists, and other critical information needed for preparing offers throughout the office.

A group arrives for a summer internship but is left without Jussi’s guidance. Something has happened to Jussi.
An important client has requested a proposal and given an additional 45 minutes to prepare the offer.

Will Your team manage to complete the proposal within the given time?



## Slide 5: How will it be organized?

The escape room will be played as part of the Basics of Financial Management course.

Group size: 3-5 people.

The games will be played during teaching weeks

Each group will play once.

The instructor, Jere Peiponen, will facilitate the escape room experience.



## Slide 6: What skills do you need?

Cost-volume-profit calculations (CVP)
Interest and percentage calculations
Teamwork
Communication skills
Creativity
Listening skills
A sense of calmness

A good attitude!
