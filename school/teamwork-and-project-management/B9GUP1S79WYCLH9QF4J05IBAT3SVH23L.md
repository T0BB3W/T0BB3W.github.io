The essence of transversal skills

1. A must read

There has been talk of generic skills and 21st-century skills in higher education. Transversal skills
are part of this discussion. They refer to skills that can be transferred to various situations and
professions. Table 1 below examines transversal skills, particularly interpersonal and emotional
skills, as well as relationship skills and their connection to the learning outcomes (Raatikainen &
Rantala-Nenonen, 2022).
