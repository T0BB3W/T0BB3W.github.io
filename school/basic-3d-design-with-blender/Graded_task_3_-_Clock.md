## Slide 4: Hints

… remember that any material below the transparent glass must have the ”Receive Transparent” tick box ticked, otherwise it will npot show under glass.
It’s a good idea to move the origin of the numbers or tick marks down from the first one, then you can rotate copies around that origin and not have to worry about alignment
Or you can use a 12-point circle to give you the locations of the numbers or ticks
