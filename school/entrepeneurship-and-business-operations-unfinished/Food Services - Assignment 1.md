## Slide 14: Wolt business operations

Wolt +: B to C subscription-based programme,                                                             allowing customers cheaper delivery costs, and deals on meals.
Wolt Drive: C-C & B-C “last-mile” delivery service for parcels and products. If your company would be needing a new phone for example, it could be delivered in under an hour using this service.

## Slide 18: Mumbai Dabbawala business operations

Service for delivering lunch boxes to office workers in Mumbai. 
Each Dabbawala (courier) has a dedicated collection and delivery area. 
Every morning, they tour their neighbourhood mainly on foot or bicycle, collecting an average of 30 lunchboxes. 
Boxes are sorted for transport at a local office or railway station to be forwarded to delivery areas by train. 
Sorting is done based on the code written on top of each box. The code indicates the exact location of where the delivery should be made.
