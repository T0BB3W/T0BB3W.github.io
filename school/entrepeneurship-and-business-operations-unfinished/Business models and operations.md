**Business models and operations**

- **objective**

  - to understand the connections between business operations and the business models that companies rely on.

  6.  **distribution networks**

  7.  **organisational structure**

  8.  **current financial performance**

  9.  **strategy for the upcoming future development**

- **statements**

  1.  **how innovative the companies are**

  2.  **whether and how much they are open to taking risks**

  3.  **open to internationalisation**

  4.  **digitalisation**

  5.  **other business trends, such as customer engagement in all stages of product development**

- **sources**
