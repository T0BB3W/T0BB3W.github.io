*Saunailta*

Matti and Maija live in an apartement that belongs to the KallionKolot housing company. Matti and Maija are in their 30’ies. THey are going to have guests next weekend and wish to offer sauna for their guests. Maija books the B2 sauna with the club room. She manages to reach the caretaker and he affirms the reservation.

Before the guests arrive Matti checks that the sauna is heated. IT is, but Matti notices that the clib room is untidy and that the reservation list on the sauna dor is not updated. Together they clean up the club room. Sauna evening with the guests runs pleasantly and luckily nobody else is trying to enter the sauna at the same time.
