**Concept clarifications**

**Personalised User Story / Narrative / Scenario**

- Personalised and detailed narrative that describes\
  **a specific single** **episode** that could occur.

**User Story / Epic (in Scrum)**

- Formal and general requirement consisting of 3 parts:\
  who – what – why.

**Product Backlog (in Scrum)**

- A list of epics in a prioritised order\
  (which may change during the project).
